As an experienced roofing contractor and sheet metal specialist Hausman Metal Works and Roofing provide the following services: commercial and industrial roofing, both new installation and roof repair, metal roofing and solar roofing.

Our services also include the manufacturing and installation of industrial sheet metal fabrication, and architectural sheet metal design.

Our years of experience with both HVAC and industrial ventilation include: large scale hospital projects with surgery suites and clean rooms, to simple commercial office ductwork. As always our projects meet or exceed SMACNA standards.

No job is too small or too large for Hausman metal works and roofing. Our dedication to high quality work and level of professionalism is virtually unmatched. Call one of our friendly and professional staff members today for a competitive bid.
