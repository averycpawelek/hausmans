Commerical and Industrial Roofing

Since 1960, Hausman metal works and roofing has been responsible for literally “covering” a large area in Northwest Missouri. Our certified safety program sets us apart from our competition. As an experienced roofing contractor, we’ve earned a reputation of being a leader in the roofing industry. We’ve provided commercial roofing to numerous industries such as: Heartland Regional Medical Center, Johnson Controls, and Wire Rope Corporation of America.

Hausman Metal Works and Roofing is a certified TAMKO, TREMCO and GARLAND roofing installer. We provide built up, as well as hot and cold process roofing. Hausman is also a certified installer for DERBIGUM roofing. Our certification to install single ply e.p.d.m. rubber-roofing systems, allows us to roof shopping centers, warehouses, and office buildings.

 

Hausman is honored to belong to both the National Roofing Contractors Association and the Midwest Roofing Contractors Association. Hausman’s quality of work is unprecedented in Northwest Missouri and the surrounding area. Committed to providing a competitive bid, our dedication to high quality work and professionalism is virtually unmatched.
