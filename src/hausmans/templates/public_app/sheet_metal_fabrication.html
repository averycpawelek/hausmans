Sheet Metal Fabrication

No sheet metal fabrication job is too small or too large for Hausman Metal Works and Roofing. Our state-of-the-art equipment enables us to create anything you desire. Because of Hausman’s extensive inventory and certified safety program you’ll receive a quality product that possesses excellent workmanship.

Our well equipped shop includes a sheer, press brakes, rollers, CNC programmable punch, dual head high definition plasma cutter, and an Omax water jet abrasive cutting system. Hausman Metal Works and Roofing has all types of MIG and TIG welders, and many types of hand held plasma cutters. We also have a complete inventory of rounds, flat bars, sheets, angles, and tubing – in hot rolled, cold rolled, galvanized, stainless steel, aluminum, copper and pre-finished sheets. We have the best tools to do your job right.
